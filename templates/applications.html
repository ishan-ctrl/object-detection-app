Applications of Object Detection

  • Smart Cities: Monitor traffic, detect litter, and analyze crowds.
  • Autonomous Vehicles: Detect cars, pedestrians, signals, and road signs in real-time.
  • Retail & Inventory: Smart shelf management, loss prevention, and self-checkout systems.
  • Surveillance & Security: Detect intrusions, weapons, or unauthorized persons.
  • Healthcare: Monitor PPE compliance, detect surgical tools, and assist in elderly care.

Applications of Garbage Detection Model

  • Smart Bins: Automatically sort waste into biodegradable, recyclable, and other categories.
  • Public Area Monitoring: Detect littering or overflowing bins in real-time.
  • Municipal Waste Audits: Analyze waste patterns and generate reports for authorities.
  • Recycling Centers: Help segregate recyclable materials on conveyor belts.
  • Household Waste Classification: Assist people via phone camera in disposing waste properly.
